पूजा साँवरिया एक हिंदी कवयित्री और लेखिका हैं, जिनकी रचनाएँ मानवीय भावनाओं की गहराई को शब्दों में पिरोती हैं — प्रेम, विरह, आत्मचिंतन और जीवन के वे पल जो अनकहे रह जाते हैं।

Pooja Sawariya is a Hindi poet and author whose work explores the quiet depths of human emotion — love, longing, reflection, and the everyday moments that shape a life. Her poetry gives language to feelings that often remain unspoken, reaching readers across India and beyond.

Her writing draws from lived experience and societal observation. Published across four collections — from her debut Samarpan to the acclaimed Ae Zindagi — her work stands as a testament to the enduring power of Hindi poetry in the contemporary literary landscape.
